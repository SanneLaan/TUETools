What is TUE Tools?

TUE Tools is an online web app that allows students from the TU/e to share their documents (summaries, example tests) to help other students prepare for their courses.

Can I add my own documents?

Yes, of course! The main aim of this web app is to help other students. By uploading your own documents we can build a decent database of documents so that future students can have a great benefit from this!
