The idea behind TUE Tools.

Users of TUE Tools can share and download files belonging to the courses they are following. Everything what comes in handy to pass a course can be uploaded via your own account. Think of books, summaries, handouts, studyguides, etc. By creating an account you will get access to all the documents other users have uploaded, and be able to upload your own documents yourself. Build a practical library full with the files you need every exam.

Courses

You can look for the courses that you are currently attending at the TU/e, or will attend in the future and mark them as 'current'. Following these courses with TUE Tools means that you will get all the documents connected to these certain courses in your personal library.

Documents

Documents are uploaded by other students, attending those courses at the TU/e. By creating an account you can maintain your own library by folowing courses and adding documents. Furthermore, you can upload documents yourself to add to your library or help other students out.
